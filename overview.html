Access Control Ontology Module

This repository contains the Ontology-Based Access Control Module (OBACM) developed as part of a master's thesis at Jönköping University. It integrates semantic web technologies to enable role- and context-aware access control for modular Digital Product Passports (DPPs), supporting data traceability, FAIR principles, and regulatory compliance in the built environment. This work is conducted as part of Digital Product Passport Systems for Sustainability (PASS4Sustainability), a research initiative funded by the Knowledge Foundation and hosted at Jönköping University. The project aims to explore how DPPs can be practically used to enable a Circular Economy (CE) across industries.

It aligns with the vision of a circular built environment by ensuring that relevant stakeholders receive the right information at the right time.
